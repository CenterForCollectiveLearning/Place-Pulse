The Place Pulse Dataset


The Place Pulse dataset is the first multi-city dataset on urban perception.

Dataset Currently Unavilable

The Place Pulse dataset and documentation will be released together with the publication of our first peer review publication.

Dataset includes:

  • ✔ 11 Urban Perception Questions
  • ✔ 58 Cities Worldwide
  • ✔ ~140,000 Geocoded Images
  • ✔ Q-Scores for All Images

Urban perception preferences were collected using our high-throughput methods. The dataset was generated using users' preferences over random pairs of geo-tagged images.

Download

The Place Pulse dataset, version 1 should be cited as:

Salesses, Schechtner and Hidalgo: Understanding the Spatial Patterns and Social Implications of Urban Perception. Under Review (2013)
