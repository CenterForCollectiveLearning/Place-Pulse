StreetScore(2014)

StreetScore is a collection of map visualizations of perceived safety of street views from cities in the United States. The maps are made by feeding a subset of the votes from the Place Pulse dataset -- 'Which place looks safer?' -- as input to a machine learning algorithm which is designed to predict how safe a street view looks to a human observer (see StreetScore FAQ).
