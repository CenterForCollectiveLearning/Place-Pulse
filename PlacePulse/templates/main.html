Learn about us

Developed by the Macro Connections group, as part of the MIT Media Lab City Science Initiative, Place Pulse is a tool to measure urban perception and study its effects on social and economic outcomes.

Learn More »

Use our data

The Place Pulse dataset is the first multi-city dataset on urban perception, collected using a high-throughput method, and is based on people’s preferences over random pairs of geo-tagged images.
