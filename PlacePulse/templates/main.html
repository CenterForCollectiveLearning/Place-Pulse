Learn about us

Developed at the MIT Media Lab by the Macro Connections group, Place Pulse crowdsources visual preference surveys to internet participants, generating perception scores that can be used by third-party applications.

Learn More »

Use our data

The Place Pulse dataset is the first multi-city dataset on urban perception, collected using a high-throughput method, and is based on people’s preferences over random pairs of geo-tagged images.
