FAQ


Answers to frequently asked questions.

  • How do I create an account?

    We have chosen to use Facebook and Mozilla's Persona as the primary methods for creating accounts. Please visit either of those sites to create a new account. Once you have an acount, return to Place Pulse and sign in by simply clicking the service of your choice.

  • How do I view the results of a study?

    Once a study has received enough votes for our ranking algorithm to produce a confident ranking, all results can be accessed by visiting the Results Page.

  • Can I download the Place Pulse dataset?

  • Have you considered referencing your data to crime or housing prices?

    Yes. We have found some interesting associations that we are sharing in our first paper on this dataset. Unfortunately, we cannot share those until the paper has been accepted for publication. As the research becomes validated by the peer review process, we will share these results on this site as well.

  • Can I create a study for any city?

    Google Street View is not available everywhere, so make sure that the area you want to study has Street View available. For more help on this, see Google's documentation.

  • Why do I see a speedometer sign instead of a Street View image?

    You love us too much. Google has limited your account. Come see us again tomorrow. For more on Street View Image limitations, please see Google's documentation.
